# Privacy Policy for Growth Social AI App

## Introduction

Growth Social AI App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

#### Health and Wellness Data
- **Health Metrics**: Weight, height, blood pressure, heart rate, sleep patterns, exercise data
- **Health Goals**: Personal health objectives and progress tracking
- **Medical Information**: Medications, allergies, medical conditions (with your explicit consent)
- **Lifestyle Data**: Diet, exercise routines, mood tracking, stress levels

#### Account Information
- **Profile Data**: Name, email address, date of birth, profile picture
- **Authentication Data**: Username, password (encrypted), authentication tokens
- **Contact Information**: Phone number, emergency contacts
- **Preferences**: App settings, notification preferences, language settings

#### Usage Data
- **App Usage**: Features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, app version
- **Location Data**: General location (with your permission) for location-based features
- **Performance Data**: App crashes, performance metrics, error logs

#### Communication Data
- **Messages**: Chat messages with friends and community members
- **Voice Data**: Voice messages and AI voice interactions
- **Social Content**: Posts, comments, shared content
- **Support Communications**: Customer service interactions

### Automatically Collected Information

#### Technical Data
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Network Information**: IP address, network type, connection speed
- **App Analytics**: Usage statistics, feature adoption, user engagement
- **Crash Reports**: Error logs and crash data for debugging

#### Cookies and Tracking
- **Session Data**: Temporary data for app functionality
- **Analytics Cookies**: Anonymous usage statistics
- **Preference Cookies**: User settings and preferences
